As we move through 2026, the shift from "Chatbots" to "AI Agents" is complete. These autonomous systems don't just talk; they act.

## What are AI Agents?
Unlike simple AI that responds to prompts, agents can plan, use tools, and execute complex workflows to achieve a goal.

## Impact on Industry
From personalized education to automated software engineering, agents are increasing productivity by orders of magnitude.

## The Ethical Frontier
With great power comes great responsibility. Ensuring these agents are safe, transparent, and aligned with human values is the biggest challenge of our decade.

Stay ahead of the curve by understanding how to collaborate with these new digital partners.
