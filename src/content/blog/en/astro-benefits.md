Astro has taken the web development world by storm with its "Zero JS by Default" philosophy. With the release of Astro 5, the framework has become even more powerful.

## The Island Architecture
Astro's unique island architecture allows you to ship mostly static HTML while only hydrating the interactive parts of your page. This leads to incredible performance and perfect SEO scores.

## Content Collections
Handling Markdown and MDX has never been easier. With type-safe content collections, you can catch errors at build time and enjoy a smooth developer experience.

## Performance First
In 2026, speed is everything. Astro ensures your site loads instantly on any device, providing a premium experience for your readers.

If you are building a blog, portfolio, or documentation site, Astro is undoubtedly the best choice today.
